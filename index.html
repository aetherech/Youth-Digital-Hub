How It Works

Three steps to your
first opportunity.

From sign-up to placement — the platform handles the complexity so you can focus on your future.

01
Step One
Build Your Profile

Create your unified digital learner profile. Upload your ID, academic results, and documents once. Our AI analyses your profile to surface relevant opportunities immediately.

  • AI-powered career quiz & matching
  • Document upload & management
  • Province and interest filtering
  • Personalised opportunity feed
02
Step Two
Apply in One Click

Apply to multiple TVET colleges, SETAs, and corporate opportunities from one centralised hub. No duplicated forms. No lost documents. Track every application in real-time.

  • Apply to bursaries, learnerships & internships
  • Application status tracking dashboard
  • SMS, email & in-app notifications
  • AI-generated CV builder
03
Step Three
Get Placed

Institutions and employers shortlist, review, and contact you directly on the platform. Get placed at a TVET college, funded through a bursary, or onboarded into a learnership.

  • Direct messaging with institutions
  • Interview & shortlist notifications
  • TVET college enrolment pipeline
  • Corporate talent placement
